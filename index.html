Protected, every step

Where the money actually sits.

Every transaction runs through escrow. Nobody gets paid — and nobody loses money — until the handoff is confirmed.

Escrowed

If you're shopping

  • 01You pay in. The full amount is held, not released, the moment you confirm a match.
  • 02Your traveler picks up the item and flies your route.
  • 03You confirm delivery — only then does escrow release the funds.
  • 04Anything goes wrong before that? You're refunded in full.
Escrowed

If you're traveling

  • 01You accept a request. The payout amount locks in escrow immediately.
  • 02You carry the item — same as anything else in your bag.
  • 03You confirm handoff with a photo and a unique code.
  • 04Payout releases to you instantly. No chasing, no waiting on invoices.

The team

Built by people who felt the problem.

BagXtra was born out of our founders' frustration with expensive, unreliable international shipping — getting everyday items from the U.S. to places where access is limited or overpriced shouldn't be this hard.

Ikem, Priyanka, and Richard teamed up to build a community-driven alternative that connects shoppers, travelers, and local vendors directly. We believe access to global goods shouldn't depend on where you live — and we're building BagXtra to make that true.
